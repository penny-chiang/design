About

Who I am

I'm a product designer based in Vancouver with 6 years of experience designing enterprise B2B software — most recently at VMware, where I was the sole designer on vSAN Data Protection for over two years.

My background is in making technically complex systems understandable — translating infrastructure concepts into interfaces that IT administrators can use confidently, even when the stakes are high (nobody wants to find out their data protection failed during a disaster).

I believe good design in enterprise software isn't about making things pretty. It's about reducing cognitive load at the moments that matter most, getting alignment across opinionated stakeholders, and having the patience to iterate until the mental model is right.

Currently

Open to remote product design roles in Canada or with North American teams. Actively learning AI product design and looking to bring my enterprise systems background into the AI space.
